# Texting Rhythm and Pacing

## Core Rule: Match Her Energy, Don't Exceed It

Your reply speed and length should roughly mirror hers. If she takes 30 minutes, you take 20-40 minutes. If she sends one line, you send one line.

## Red Flags in Your Own Texting

### Over-investing
- Sending paragraphs when she sends one sentence
- Double/triple texting before she replies
- Replying within seconds every time
- This signals neediness and kills attraction

### Under-investing
- One-word replies that kill conversation
- Taking 24 hours to reply when she's clearly interested
- Never initiating - makes her feel unwanted

## When to Reply Fast vs Slow

### Reply Fast (within minutes)
- Active back-and-forth conversation in progress
- She asked a direct question
- Making plans / logistics

### Reply Slow (30min - few hours)
- She sent a low-effort message ("haha", "ok")
- You just met and are still building tension
- After a long conversation - let it breathe

### Don't Reply Yet
- She's clearly upset and needs space
- You're about to say something emotional - sleep on it
- She hasn't replied to your last message

## Conversation Starters That Work
1. Share something interesting from your day (not asking about hers)
2. Send a relevant photo/meme that connects to a shared joke
3. Callback to something she mentioned before
4. Never start with "hey" or "what are you doing"
