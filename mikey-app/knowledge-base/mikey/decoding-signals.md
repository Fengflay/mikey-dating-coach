# Decoding Her Signals

## High Interest Signals
- Replies quickly and with substance
- Asks questions about you
- Uses lots of emojis/exclamation marks
- Brings up future plans ("we should...")
- Shares personal stories unprompted
- Sends photos of her day/selfies
- Texts you first regularly

## Medium Interest / Testing
- Replies but doesn't ask questions back
- Takes varying amounts of time to reply
- Short but not cold replies
- "Haha" + something vs just "haha"
- Responds to your stories but doesn't initiate

## Low Interest / Fading
- One-word replies: "ok", "haha", "sure"
- Takes increasingly long to reply
- Never initiates conversation
- Doesn't acknowledge questions
- Generic responses that could go to anyone
- "I'm busy" without suggesting another time

## The Testing Framework
Women often "test" men to gauge confidence and emotional stability:

### Compliance Tests
She asks you to do something unreasonable to see if you'll bend.
- Strategy: Say no with humor, don't get angry

### Congruence Tests
She challenges something you said to see if you'll back down.
- Strategy: Hold your frame calmly, don't over-explain

### Investment Tests
She pulls back to see if you'll chase.
- Strategy: Match her energy level, don't pursue harder

## Critical Rule
When in doubt about her interest level: look at her ACTIONS, not her words. If she says she's interested but never makes time for you, believe the actions.
